Let the show begin!

Wriggling ear clown

For the three next shows, our fabulous Wriggling ear clown will show you his incredible talent. You might be wondering « what does this all mean ? »… In fact, his talent is based on the incredible ability to move his ears as easily as you can move your hand ! Doubters will call it magic, while scientists name this ability as « Facial gymnastics ». Come and discover the incredible focus needed to perform these amazing tricks .

  • Up and down moves
  • Left and right moves
  • Side to side juggling
  • Drawing custom shapes by moving ears

An indomitable beast

Discover our indomitable beast duo show, starring Django, a huge westie dog, escorted by his clown mistress. Django has been trained from his earliest days. If you think that you have already seen all about dog training, this show his for you, you will not believe what you see… Have you ever talked to a dog ? Oh, wait, dogs can’t speak ? Well, django can !

  • Reverse alphabet reciting in less than 5 seconds
  • Hold a political debate with François Hollande about last 10 years french economy
  • Peel 5 potatoes in 5 minutes

The crazy pastry chef

Our crazy pastry chef is a sugar lover. He spends his days cooking pies, cakes, cupcakes, and about everything which can be made with sugar. Meanwhile, you have to be careful. Our clown is mad. Don’t bother him unless you want to be one of his moving and living targets for his upcoming cake battle.

  • Juggling with cupcakes
  • Making a huge 5 meters cake in 60 seconds
  • Making cupcakes with his mouth
